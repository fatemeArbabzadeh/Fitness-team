Key Takeaways: Caffeine Before Workout Is It Recommended?
  • Impact of Caffeine on Exercise Performance:
    • Caffeine is a stimulant that can improve focus, endurance, and performance during workouts by reducing the perception of effort and increasing adrenaline levels.
  • Recommended Dosages:
    • Optimal dosages vary but typically range from 3 to 6 mg per kilogram of body weight, taken roughly 30-60 minutes before exercise. Individual tolerance levels should be considered.
  • Benefits and Risks:
    • Benefits include increased alertness, fat utilization for energy, and improved endurance. Risks may involve jitteriness, insomnia, or increased heart rate if consumed in excess.
  • Incorporating Caffeine:
    • To maximize its benefits, consider timing caffeine intake strategically, staying hydrated, and being mindful of any potential interactions with other supplements or medications.
